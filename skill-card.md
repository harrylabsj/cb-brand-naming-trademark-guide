## Description: <br>
Guides teams through generating and evaluating brand or product names across languages with preliminary cultural, domain, and trademark-readiness screening. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[harrylabsj](https://clawhub.ai/user/harrylabsj) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External founders, brand teams, product marketers, and localization managers use this skill to turn a naming brief into screened name candidates, scorecards, and shortlist comparison matrices for target markets. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Trademark, domain, and cultural screening may be mistaken for legal clearance. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as preliminary business research and use qualified trademark counsel before adopting a name commercially. <br>
Risk: Confidential launch names or strategy details could be exposed through prompts or generated artifacts. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid sharing confidential names unless the execution environment is approved for that information. <br>
Risk: Cross-language and cultural checks can miss local meanings, slang, or sensitivities. <br>
Mitigation: Validate shortlisted names with local linguistic, cultural, and market reviewers before launch. <br>
